# HRUDAY Industries | bamboopores

**Hemalata Research Industry for Universal Development and Agro-economic Yield**

Located in Dumuriput, Koraput, HRUDAY Industries is a research-driven manufacturing unit 
dedicated to the full-utilization of bamboo species (*Bambusa nutans* and *Dendrocalamus strictus*).

## 🔬 Our Philosophy: The "Bamboopore"
We analyze the vascular bundle structure (pores) of bamboo to optimize:
- **Filtration:** High-surface-area Activated Charcoal.
- **Strength:** Precision-bonded Bamboo Composite Boards.
- **Purity:** Bio-active Bamboo Vinegar via controlled pyrolysis.
- **Absorption:** Premium Incense Sticks (Agarbatti) and Masala.

## 🌿 Sustainability
Operating on a **Zero-Waste Model**, we ensure every fiber of the bamboo is utilized, 
supporting the local tribal economy of Odisha while maintaining a carbon-neutral footprint.
